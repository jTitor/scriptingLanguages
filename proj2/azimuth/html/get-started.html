Azimuth User Guide

Azimuth consists of two forms: one for basic student information, and the second for course selection. To begin the course advising process, first enter your UMBC student information.

Next, a list of courses specifc to your major will be provided. Check off the courses you have passed or will pass before the next semester.

Finally, Azimuth will display a results page of courses you are eligible to take.

Click the “Start Advising” button to begin.
